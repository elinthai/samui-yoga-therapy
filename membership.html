What membership is

One scan.
One session.
Every month.

The monthly fee is ฿2,500. That is the cost of one Styku body scan and one private session — paid as a standing commitment. You book it like any other session. The difference is it happens every month without negotiation, without deciding each time, without letting the practice drift.

Members also get discounted rates on everything else the studio offers — additional sessions, extra scans, packages. The deeper you go, the better the rate.

What membership opens

Beyond the sessions and the rates — the things you can't book as a drop-in.

Priority access

Morning slots, last-minute availability, and first right of refusal when the schedule opens. The studio is small. Members book first.

Community

Run club. Hikes. Sound events. Studio classes. Members are the people Eli calls when something is happening on the island. Not an email list — actual plans.

Newsletter

What's being built, what's being learned, what's next. Honest and infrequent — when there's something worth saying, not to fill a schedule.

Retreats & events

Studio retreats, collaborative events, and any session that happens outside the regular schedule. Members hear about these first and get first access.

Jungle Village — inside access

What's being built on the island. Members get early visibility into retreat availability, community projects, and Stage Two of Jungle Village before anything is public.

The relationship

Membership isn't a subscription. It's the formalisation of a relationship that already exists. If you've done the sessions and want it to continue — this is how.

Where this leads

Something bigger is being built on this island.

Jungle Village is a regenerative community in progress on Koh Samui — grown in stages, grounded in the same practice that runs through the studio. Samui Yoga Therapy and Body Sculpt Samui are its operating foundation.

Members are the inner circle of what comes next. Not investors — though some will be. People who are close enough to the work to know what's being built, and why, before anyone else does.
