What ten sessions means

Not a pass. A commitment to a direction — with someone who tracks where you started and where you're going.

Your pace

Use them your way

Ten sessions, valid for three months. Some clients use all ten in two weeks — intensive, focused, transformative. Others spread them across a full season, building a practice that outlasts their stay.

Your sessions

Built from your data

Each session sequences from where you started — your scan baseline, your injury history, what shifted last time. Nothing repeats for the sake of it. Every session has a reason.

Your practice

One practitioner, always

Same person, every session. No handoffs, no templates, no starting over. Continuity is the point — the practice compounds because the practitioner remembers.

What we work with

Sessions are built from whatever the body needs that day. Every modality below is in the room.

Vinyasa & Power

Strength, mobility, and flowing movement. For bodies that want to build and feel capable.

Yin

Deep tissue release, recovery, nervous system reset. For bodies that need to let something go.

Ashtanga

Traditional, progressive, disciplined. For the practitioner who wants structure and a lineage to follow.

Breathwork & Meditation

Steadiness for the nervous system. Works alone or woven through any session.

Sound Healing

Vibration, stillness, restoration. Often where ten sessions ends — a full hour of nothing but rest.

Strength & Mobility

Functional, practical, resilient. Movement built to last in the body beyond the studio.
